MOTHER THERESA INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Mother Theresa Institute of Engineering and Technology, was established in the year 2010 with the objective of empowering the rural youth for
whom engineering education was not attainable easily. The Institute is promoted by a non-profitable organization, ‘Holy Cross Educational Society, Palamaner.’ The inception of MTIET has been based on the commitment to impart holistic technical education to the rural and under privileged sections of the society.
